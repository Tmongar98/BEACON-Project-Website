What is BEACON?

Bicycle Electronic Awareness and Communication On-road Network, or "BEACON", is an all-in-one bicycle-mounted safety system. Designed to improve rider visibility, safety, and communication with other nearby drivers and pedestrians. Containing an automatic brake light system, left and right turning signals through handlebar controls, an onboard display for rider analytics, and a rapidly approaching rear-object warning system.
